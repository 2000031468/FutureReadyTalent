About us

Dream Brics is a premium luxury and high end real estate agency that caters to the upper end of the residential and commercial sale and leasing market in Mumbai. Our approach is based on professionalism, trust and respect for the consumer. We have an able team of young and dynamic agents and professionals with a diverse set of experiences and skills in order to best leverage our position as a premium and respectable brand in the real estate consultancy space in India. The team is founded by Amit Sen & Atin Dasgupta, two highly experienced professionals from Related Industries. The company was formed in the year 2014 to cater to the growing need for quality educated professionals and to address the trust deficit and adequate due diligence gaps in the real estate agency space in India. We are proud to say that we have quickly climbed our way to the very top echelons of the luxury real estate market and aim to be the first truly respected global real estate advisory firm from India.
